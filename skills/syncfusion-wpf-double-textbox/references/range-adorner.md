# Range Adorner in WPF Double TextBox

The range adorner visually indicates the value as a filled region (like a progress bar). Enable with `EnableRangeAdorner`. Adorner displays only when `MinValue` and `MaxValue` are set.

## Changing background of range-adorner
Use `RangeAdornerBackground` to color the adorner.
